Jess Martínez

She's single, bilingual, and ready to mingle... Well, she's single. Jess Martínez is a Chicago-based comedian who enjoys making audiences laugh as she genuinely tries to make sense of the world around her.

She is the Associate Producer of Las Locas Comedy and an Assistant Producer at The Moth StorySLAM Chicago.

Jess is a graduate of both The Second City Training Center and Feminine Comique stand-up programs.
